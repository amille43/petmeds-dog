Antibiotics for Dogs — Basics

Antibiotics treat bacterial infections, not viruses. Accurate diagnosis and dosing are key to avoid resistance and side effects.

What to discuss with your vet

  • Culture & sensitivity when appropriate
  • Correct dosing by weight and duration
  • Common side effects (GI upset), when to stop and call

Never use leftover antibiotics or share medications between pets.
