Pain Relief (NSAIDs) — Safety First

Veterinary NSAIDs can help with pain and inflammation but require correct dosing and monitoring.

Checklist

  • Baseline labwork for long‑term use
  • Watch for vomiting, black stools, appetite loss
  • Do not combine NSAIDs unless directed by your vet
